Introduction #

The Vape Review Hub (the “Site”) provides independent reviews and guides for adult readers (21+). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit the Site, read our content, use the search, or contact us via the contact form. By using the Site, you agree to this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use.

This page is provided for transparency and does not constitute legal advice.

Information we collect #

Information you provide

  • Contact form details: name, email, message content, and selected topic.
  • Corrections & tips: any information you choose to include in your message.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data: pages viewed, referring/exit pages, approximate location (city/country), device/browser type, and timestamps.
  • Cookies & similar technologies: small files used for preferences, analytics, and performance. See Cookies & tracking.
  • Server logs: IP address and user‑agent recorded briefly for security and troubleshooting.

Your privacy choices & rights #

Depending on your location, you may have rights such as access, correction, deletion, portability, objection, or restriction. We honor valid requests regardless of where you live, within reasonable limits.

Make a request

  1. Describe your request (access, delete, correct, etc.) and include the email you used with us.
  2. Submit via our contact form. We may ask you to verify ownership of the email address.

Privacy choices

  • Cookie preferences: You can update consent choices at any time using the controls referenced on this page or by clearing cookies in your browser. See Manage cookies.
  • Analytics: You may opt out by disabling analytics cookies. We use aggregate reporting where possible.
  • Do Not Sell or Share: We do not sell your personal information. If you have questions, contact us via the contact form.
  • Do Not Track: Most browsers offer a “DNT” setting. While industry standards are evolving, we limit tracking to functional and analytics uses described here.

Data retention #

We keep information only as long as needed for the purposes described above:

  • Contact form messages: typically up to 24 months for follow‑up and quality assurance.
  • Server logs: typically less than 30 days unless needed for security investigations.
  • Analytics data: aggregate reports may be stored longer without identifying individuals.

Data security #

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, including HTTPS transport, limited access, and provider security practices. No system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children & age restrictions #

This Site is for adults (21+) and is not intended for minors. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 21. If you believe a minor has provided information, please contact us and we will delete it.

International transfers #

Our providers may process information in multiple countries. We rely on appropriate safeguards (such as contractual commitments) when information is transferred across borders.

Affiliate disclosure #

Some articles may include clearly marked affiliate links to retailers. If you click and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. Affiliate partners may use a cookie to record that a referral originated from this Site. See Cookies & tracking.

Manage cookies #

  1. Site controls: Use the cookie banner or preference controls when presented. You can revisit your choices by clearing cookies and reloading the Site.
  2. Browser settings: Most browsers let you block or delete cookies, or enable settings like “block third‑party cookies.”
  3. Private browsing: Use an incognito/private window for session‑only cookies.

Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.
