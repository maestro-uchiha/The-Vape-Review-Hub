Corrections & feedback #

See something off? We appreciate careful readers. Send the page URL, the exact text in question, and any supporting sources. Substantive errors are corrected promptly; minor edits may be silent.

  • Include screenshots for labeling or packaging claims.
  • We list material corrections within the article where appropriate.

Pitches & samples #

We purchase most products anonymously. Unsolicited samples may be declined. If you wish to pitch a product for review, share what’s new, your distribution regions, and any safety documentation.

  • Sending a sample does not guarantee coverage or a favorable review.
  • All coverage follows our independent policy; see About for details.
