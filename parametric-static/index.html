How we test devices #

We combine long‑term daily use with bench checks. Our scoring looks at build quality, flavor accuracy, coil lifespan, battery health, and labeling transparency.

  1. Baselines: We run standardized e‑liquids and record puff curves at fixed power settings.
  2. Leak audits: 72‑hour heat/cold cycling in sealed bags to detect seepage.
  3. Battery checks: Charge/discharge cycles with logged mAh and observed voltage sag.
  4. Label verification: Compare stated nicotine strength and capacity to measured results when lab data is available.
  5. User experience: Mouthfeel, draw consistency, refill ease, and maintenance costs over 30 days.

Safety and compliance #

21+ only. Nicotine is an addictive chemical. This site is for adult readers. We do not sell products.

Health note: If you do not smoke, don’t start vaping. Consider evidence‑based resources from public‑health organizations such as the CDC and FDA.

Battery safety: Use quality chargers, carry cells in cases, and replace damaged wraps. See our guides above for more.
