Our mission #

The Vape Review Hub exists to help adult vapers (21+) make smarter, safer choices. We prioritize clear information, transparent testing, and practical guidance over hype and marketing claims. We do not sell products.

  • Independence: Reviews are written without manufacturer approval.
  • Clarity: We translate specs and lab data into plain‑language takeaways.
  • Responsibility: If you don’t smoke, don’t start vaping. See our Terms for site usage and our Privacy Policy for data practices.

Editorial policy & ethics #

We purchase most devices anonymously. When samples are provided, we disclose that fact and apply the same test protocol. Brands do not receive copy approval or score influence, and we decline paid “guaranteed positive” placements.

  1. Evidence first: Claims must be backed by repeatable observations or reputable sources.
  2. Corrections: If we miss something, we correct the page and note substantive changes.
  3. Conflicts: Any potential conflicts are disclosed within the article.
  4. User safety: We highlight battery and e‑liquid safety cues in every review.

How we’re funded #

Our work is supported by reader trust. Some articles may include clearly marked affiliate links. If you click and buy, we may earn a commission|at no additional cost to you. Affiliate partnerships never change our verdicts, rankings, or coverage.

  • We do not run sponsored “reviews.”
  • We do not sell ad placements that affect scoring.
  • We choose products based on reader interest and testing capacity.

Our testing process #

Each device goes through real‑world use and bench checks. We evaluate draw quality, flavor accuracy, coil lifespan, leak resistance, charging behavior, and labeling transparency. For deeper methodology notes and category weights, see guides on the blog.

Daily use

30‑day checks for reliability, maintenance costs, and user experience.

Bench tests

Puff curve logging, capacity verification (where feasible), and charge/discharge tracking.

Safety cues

Clear warnings, protections, and prompts are noted and scored.
